Hadamard gate

The Hadamard gate creates superposition:

$$ |0\rangle \to |+\rangle $$

$$ |1\rangle \to |-\rangle $$

It maps Z-basis $\leftrightarrow$ X-basis and is key for generating entanglement.

The H-gate can also be seen as a 180$^\circ$ rotation about the axis defined by the vector $\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(1,0,1)$.
